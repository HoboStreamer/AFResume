A community-editable survival resource map at [maps.hobo.tools](https://maps.hobo.tools). Part of the Hobo Network, sharing the same unified account and theme system. Contributors can add and verify locations for camps, free resources, rest areas, water sources, shelters, and public facilities across North America.
